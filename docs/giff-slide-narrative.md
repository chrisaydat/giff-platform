# GIFF Demo Slide Narrative

## Narrative Thesis

Semper Fidum is proposing a unified institutional operating platform for GIFF, not a disconnected member portal. The story should show that the platform serves two audiences at once:

- members who need a trustworthy self-service experience for renewals, dues, documents, and standing
- GIFF executives and staff who need operational visibility across assessment, membership, billing, accounts, HR, migration, integrations, and training

The demo should therefore be presented as one connected system with two role-specific surfaces.

## Suggested Deck Flow

### Slide 1: Title

- Working title: `GIFF Institutional Operating Platform`
- Core message: A modern operating platform for membership administration, financial oversight, compliance visibility, and institutional service delivery.
- Visual proof: Gateway screen or a composed split visual of the member portal and executive console.
- Speaker note: Open with the shift from fragmented administration to a single digital archive and operating layer.

### Slide 2: GIFF's Operating Challenge

- Core message: GIFF is managing membership, renewals, dues, compliance records, finance, staff processes, and member communication across fragmented workflows.
- Visual proof: Simple problem frame, not UI-heavy.
- Speaker note: Position the challenge as operational fragmentation, manual review, slow traceability, and limited self-service.

### Slide 3: Our Response

- Core message: We designed one platform with two synchronized experiences.
- Visual proof:
  - Member Portal
  - Executive Console
- Speaker note: The member side reduces friction for institutions, while the executive side gives GIFF control, traceability, and delivery oversight.

### Slide 4: Member Experience

- Core message: Members can see standing, complete renewals, settle dues, download records, receive alerts, and manage profile/security in one place.
- Demo proof:
  - `/dashboard`
  - `/membership`
  - `/dues`
  - `/documents`
  - `/notifications`
  - `/settings`
  - `/profile`
- Speaker note: Emphasize clarity, trust, and reduced back-and-forth with the secretariat.

### Slide 5: Renewal and Compliance Flow

- Core message: Renewal is not just form submission; it is a structured standing review tied to documentation, dues, and compliance.
- Demo proof:
  - member renewal stepper on `/membership`
  - compliance archive and credential status on `/profile`
  - supporting alerts on `/notifications`
- Speaker note: This is where the platform begins replacing manual review cycles with visible workflow states.

### Slide 6: Dues, Receipts, and Digital Records

- Core message: The platform centralizes invoices, payments, receipts, letters, and verifiable member documents.
- Demo proof:
  - `/dues`
  - `/dues/[id]`
  - `/documents`
- Speaker note: Stress trust and auditability: a member can prove status, retrieve records, and verify institutional documentation without secretariat intervention.

### Slide 7: Executive Operating Console

- Core message: GIFF staff and leadership gain a coordinated operating view across the full requested scope.
- Demo proof:
  - `/executive`
  - `/executive/assessment`
  - `/executive/members`
  - `/executive/fees`
  - `/accounts`
  - `/hr`
  - `/audit`
- Speaker note: This slide should show that the demo is broader than member UX; it addresses the institutional operating model.

### Slide 8: Scope Coverage Against the EOI

- Core message: Every major workstream requested in the EOI is represented in the demo.
- Scope checklist:
  - Assessment and Analysis
  - Membership Management
  - Dues and Fees Administration
  - Accounts
  - Human Resource Management
  - Integrations and secure data governance
  - Data Migration
  - Training, Support, and Maintenance
- Demo proof:
  - `/executive/assessment`
  - `/executive/migration`
  - `/executive/integrations`
  - `/executive/training`
- Speaker note: This is the slide where we explicitly prove scope compliance.

### Slide 9: Migration, Integration, and Governance

- Core message: We are not presenting a greenfield interface only; we are presenting a realistic transition path.
- Demo proof:
  - migration monitoring and exception handling on `/executive/migration`
  - integration and control surfaces on `/executive/integrations`
  - audit visibility on `/audit`
- Speaker note: Reassure GIFF that adoption, data integrity, and operational governance were considered from the start.

### Slide 10: Training, Support, and Adoption

- Core message: Rollout succeeds when staff can operate the system confidently after launch.
- Demo proof:
  - `/executive/training`
  - support touchpoints on member settings and notifications
- Speaker note: Link this to onboarding, help resources, hypercare, maintenance, and post-launch continuity.

### Slide 11: Delivery Approach

- Core message: We would deliver this in phases, starting with assessment and moving through build, migration, and adoption.
- Recommended phase framing:
  - Phase 1: Assessment
  - Phase 2: Build
  - Phase 3: Migration
  - Phase 4: Adoption and support
- Demo proof: Implementation programme on `/executive`
- Speaker note: Keep this operational and credible. This is where the final bid should sharpen timeline, staffing, and governance language.

### Slide 12: Why Semper Fidum

- Core message: We understand both the member service layer and the institutional operating layer required to modernize GIFF.
- Suggested proof points:
  - scope-aligned platform thinking
  - role-based operational design
  - implementation realism, not just visuals
  - readiness for migration, governance, and staff enablement
- Speaker note: Once the submitted bid is added, this slide should absorb company credentials, team composition, and delivery differentiators.

## Live Demo Order

If the presentation includes a live walk-through, use this order:

1. Gateway at `/`
2. Member dashboard at `/dashboard`
3. Renewal flow at `/membership`
4. Dues and receipt view at `/dues`
5. Documents and profile trust signals at `/documents` and `/profile`
6. Switch to executive overview at `/executive`
7. Membership operations at `/executive/members`
8. Dues administration at `/executive/fees`
9. Migration, integrations, and training at `/executive/migration`, `/executive/integrations`, and `/executive/training`
